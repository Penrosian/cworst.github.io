Ingredients

Ingredients
Ingredients amount
Hot Water 1 cup
Packet of Jello 1
Cold water 1 cup
refridgerator 1
place in refridgerator 2-3 hours

Instructions

  1. First boil a cup of water (add more water to account for the water that may be evaporated)
  2. put jelllo mix in a container (prefferably glass)
  3. mix hot water and jello mix together until the jello mix is dissolved.
  4. once mix is dissolved you would stir the cold water in
  5. after the cold water is mixed in you would place in refridgerator for 2-3 hours
